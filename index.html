Do's & Don'ts
✅ Add something new
Share a related experience or insight. Comments that extend the conversation get the most replies.
✅ End with a genuine question
This triggers a reply from the author — LinkedIn boosts the post and you get noticed.
✅ Comment in the first 30–60 mins
Early comments get the most visibility. Check LinkedIn 2× daily and jump on fresh posts fast.
❌ Never say "Great post!"
Adds zero value. Signals you didn't read it. Gets ignored 100% of the time.
❌ Don't self-promote
Never drop your CV or services unless asked. Kills the connection on the spot.
❌ Don't sound like a press release
If it sounds polished and formal, rewrite it. Real beats professional on LinkedIn.

Connection Strategy
Step 1 — Comment first, connect second
Your comment creates context — they'll recognize your name when the request arrives.
Step 2 — Personalize the connection note
"Hi [Name], I commented on your post about [topic] — would love to connect."
Step 3 — Engage 3× before DM'ing
Comment on 2–3 posts over a week. By then you're a familiar face, not a stranger.
Step 4 — Target 10 comments a day
10 genuine comments/day = ~70 touchpoints/week = fast network growth.
